Get involved with PHP London

There are lots of ways to get involved with the London PHP community.

1

Come along to a meetup

The best way to learn from and support London's PHP community is to come along to one our monthly meetups.

2

Join our Meetup.com group

Stay up-to-date with announcements from PHP London and help us track meetup attendance by joining our group on meetup.com.

3

Find us on IRC

We're #phplondon on Freenode.

4

Follow us on Twitter

For announcements, questions and other feedback you can follow and tweet us @phplondon
